# Literature Review

Purpose
- Collect, annotate, and synthesize the scholarly and technical literature that motivates the project.

Suggested contents
- references.bib or references.md — at least 10 curated references with annotations
- notes/ — literature notes and summaries
- review-summary.md — synthesize key takeaways and gaps in the literature

Formatting & citation
- Provide citations in BibTeX and a short annotated bibliography (1–3 sentences per reference) explaining relevance to the project.
